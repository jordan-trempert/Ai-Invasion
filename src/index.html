AI Invasion

How to Play

Welcome to AI Invasion! This game is based off of DougDoug's ai invasion streams!

  • Player's will take turns setting the capital for their empire.
  • After both players set a capital, they can start making moves.
  • A move can be literally anything the player wants. Just type it in the box and hit sumbit!
  • The goal is to obtain 24 of the 48 available states.

AI is stupid so play by these rules:

  • Player's can only conquer a state if they own a state adjacent to it.
  • The AI will (attempt to) output a scenario based on the player's action. It is up to the players to decided if they succeeded or failed.
  • If player's cannot come to a consensus, do a coin flip.
  • Each player gets the ability to collect 3 special resources.
  • There are two different ways player's can choose to use them:
  • 1. Player's use one of their turns attempting to collect a resource (this is the preferred play-style).
  • 2. Each player starts the game with 3 special resources of their choosing.

Have fun playing!
